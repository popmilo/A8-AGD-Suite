		   (PA1) Bit 1=Serial DATA IN
		   (PA2) Bit 2=Joy 0
		   (PA3) Bit 3=Joy 1
		   (PA4) Bit 4=Joy 2
		   (PA5) Bit 5 = Lightpen/Fire button
		   (PA6) Bit 6=Cassette switch sense
		   (PA7) Bit 7=Serial ATN out
